About Us

Our story, our passion, our family

Our Story

La Casa de Sabor opened its doors in 2009 with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of Mexico to our community. Founded by the Rodriguez family, our restaurant has been a labor of love, combining traditional recipes passed down through generations with the warmest hospitality.

What started as a small family dream has grown into a beloved local establishment, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every dish we serve represents our commitment to quality, authenticity, and the rich culinary heritage of Mexico.

Our Mission

At La Casa de Sabor, we believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's a way to bring people together, to celebrate, and to create memories. Our mission is to provide an authentic Mexican dining experience that transports you to the vibrant streets of Mexico.

We're committed to using only the freshest ingredients, preparing each dish with care, and serving our guests with genuine warmth and hospitality. When you dine with us, you're not just a customer—you're family.

Our Values

Fresh & Local

We source our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, ensuring the highest quality and supporting our community.

Traditional Recipes

Our recipes have been perfected over generations, maintaining the authentic flavors of traditional Mexican cuisine.

Family First

As a family-owned restaurant, we treat every guest like part of our extended family, with warmth and care.

Quality Always

We never compromise on quality, from the ingredients we use to the service we provide.
